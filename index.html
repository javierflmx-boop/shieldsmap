Our Mission
Built by a contractor.
For every contractor
left unpaid.

Every day across the US, hardworking contractors complete jobs in good faith and never receive payment. They have no warning. No protection. No way to know it happened before them.

ShieldsMap was created to change that. When a contractor experiences a payment dispute, they can report that property. The report is reviewed by our admin team before it goes live — ensuring accuracy and fairness.

No contractor should lose their savings, their business, or their health because one client refused to honor an agreement.

Legal Notice All reports reflect the personal experiences of verified contractor members. Every report is reviewed before publication. ShieldsMap operates under Section 230 of the Communications Decency Act.
