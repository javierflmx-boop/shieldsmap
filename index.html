Our Mission
Built by a contractor.
For every contractor
who was left unpaid.

Every day across the United States, hardworking contractors — electricians, plumbers, painters, roofers, general contractors — complete jobs in good faith and never receive payment. They have no warning. No protection. No way to know it happened to the contractor before them.

ShieldsMap was created to change that. When a contractor experiences a payment dispute, they can report that property. The report is reviewed by our admin team before it ever goes live — ensuring accuracy and fairness. Future contractors can search any address before accepting a job and make an informed decision.

This is not a blacklist. This is a community of professionals protecting each other — the same way a neighborhood watch protects a community. Green properties celebrate clients who treat contractors with respect. Yellow properties flag payment delays worth noting. Red properties document serious, unresolved disputes.

No contractor should lose their savings, their business, or their health because one client refused to honor an agreement. ShieldsMap exists so that never has to happen again.
